Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Getting Started

How do I get started with ReddrPulse?
Simply sign up for free, enter your website URL, and our AI will automatically analyze your business. You'll start seeing opportunities within minutes. No credit card required.
What information do I need to provide?
Just your website URL. Our AI will analyze your site to understand your niche, products, and target audience. You can also add details about your brand voice for more personalized content.
How long does it take to see results?
Most users see their first brand mentions within 24-48 hours. Significant results typically appear within 2-4 weeks of consistent use.
Do I need Reddit accounts?
No! ReddrPulse provides access to our network of trusted, aged Reddit accounts. You don't need to buy or manage any accounts yourself.

Features & Capabilities

What exactly does ReddrPulse do?
ReddrPulse finds Reddit threads where your potential customers are looking for solutions, generates AI-powered responses that naturally mention your brand, and posts them using our trusted accounts. It also boosts engagement to maximize visibility.
How does the AI generate comments?
Our AI analyzes each thread's context and generates multiple response variations that are helpful, relevant, and match your brand voice. You can review and approve each comment before posting.
Can I customize the AI's output?
Yes! You can train the AI on your brand voice, specify tone preferences, and even create custom response templates for different scenarios.
What subreddits does ReddrPulse target?
We automatically identify the most relevant subreddits for your niche. You can also add custom subreddits to target and exclude any that don't fit your strategy.
How does the boosting feature work?
Our smart boosting system upvoted your comments naturally to help them climb to the top of discussions. It's designed to trigger Reddit's algorithm without triggering spam filters.
Can I track my results?
Yes! The dashboard shows total comments, brand mentions, upvotes, click-throughs, and ROI. You can also set up weekly automated reports.

Pricing & Billing

Is there a free plan?
Yes! Our Starter plan is free forever. You get access to core features and pay-per-task for comments, posts, and upvotes.
What happens in the free trial?
All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. You get full access to all features with no credit card required.
Can I change my plan later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact our support team for a full refund if you're not satisfied.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Technical & Security

Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your data. Your brand information and analytics are 100% private.
Will my accounts get banned?
Our accounts are pre-warmed, have genuine karma, and use dedicated IPs. Combined with our safety score system, ban risk is virtually eliminated.
Do you offer an API?
Yes! Our Max plan includes full API access. Contact our sales team for custom API solutions.
What integrations do you support?
We integrate with Google Analytics, HubSpot, Slack, Mailchimp, and Zapier. More integrations coming soon!
